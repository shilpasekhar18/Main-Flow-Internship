Chilaquiles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 corn tortillas, cut into wedges
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 2 cups of salsa verde (green tomatillo sauce)
  • 1/2 cup crumbled queso fresco (Mexican cheese)
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs (optional, for serving)
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat and fry the tortilla wedges until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  2. In the same pan, heat the salsa verde and add the crispy tortillas. Stir gently to coat them with the sauce.
  3. Top with crumbled queso fresco, sour cream, onions, and cilantro.
  4. If desired, fry eggs separately and place them on top of the chilaquiles.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with more cilantro and onions.
